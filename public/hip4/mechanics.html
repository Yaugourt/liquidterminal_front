Contest lifecycle
  • 1
    OWNER calls createContest(contestId, entryFee) — creates the market and sets the exact entry fee in wei.
  • 2
    Anyone deposits exactly entryFee HYPE by sending a tx to the contract — emits DepositReceived(contestId, sideId, depositor, amount). 1 deposit max per address.
  • 3
    The deposit deadline passes — deposits are closed ("Deposit deadline passed").
  • 4
    OWNER publishes the winners Merkle root via publishMerkleRoot(contestId, root) — proves winners off-chain, root posted on-chain. Can only be published once.
  • 5
    Winners call claim(contestId, sideId, proof[]) with their Merkle proof — the contract verifies the proof and pays the reward. Platform fee of 0.9% deducted from the pool.
  • 6
    After the claim period: OWNER calls sweepUnclaimed(contestId) to recover unclaimed funds ("Claim period not expired" if too early).
  • !
    Alternative: before the Merkle root is published, OWNER can call refund(contestId, sideId, user) to cancel and refund a contest.
