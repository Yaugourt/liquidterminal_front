Permissionless market creation, secured by stake

HIP-3 lets any builder deploy a perpetual market on Hyperliquid. A deployer stakes 500k HYPE, wins a dutch-auction slot, runs the oracle for the market, and earns a share of the fees it generates. It turns the exchange into permissionless market infrastructure: commodities, equities, indices, anything that has a price can be listed by someone other than the core team.

Because deployment is open, the surface of what trades on Hyperliquid grows without a gatekeeper, while the stake requirement and auction keep low-quality markets from crowding the book. For a reader coming from HIP-1 and HIP-2, HIP-3 is the step where the standard stops being about listing assets and starts being about listing entire markets.
