HIP-3 : Builder-Deployed Perpetuals

Permissionless market creation, secured by stake

Hyperliquid Improvement Proposals (HIPs) are the binding code upgrades that extend the protocol. Each HIP introduces a new primitive : token standards, liquidity bootstrapping, permissionless market creation. HIP-3 is the most consequential: it opens perpetual deployment to any builder willing to put capital at stake.

A deployment slot is won through a 31-hour dutch auction. The winning builder posts the stake, operates the market's oracle, and collects half of all fees it generates. Slashing keeps oracles honest: misreport prices and the stake is at risk. Within one quarter, builder-deployed markets grew to 40% of Hyperliquid volume : led by commodities.
