HIP-3: Builder-Deployed Perpetuals

Permissionless market creation, secured by stake

HIP-3 opens perpetual market creation to anyone. A builder locks 500,000 HYPE as stake, then competes for a deployment slot in a dutch auction where the price falls until a bidder accepts it. Winning the slot grants the right to list and operate a new perpetual market on Hyperliquid.

The builder is not only an issuer, it runs the market. It publishes the oracle price the market settles against, sets the contract parameters, and stays accountable through its stake, which is at risk if the market is operated in bad faith. In return, the builder keeps half of the fees the market generates.

Builder fee share
50%
of trading fees is kept by the builder on every HIP-3 market it deploys and operates.

Because the price feed is the builder's responsibility, HIP-3 is not limited to crypto pairs. Commodities, equities and other real-world benchmarks can be listed as markets. That flexibility is why builder-deployed perps have grown into a large share of activity on Hyperliquid, past $25B in cumulative volume.
