Seat available

One sponsor. No second logo, no ad network, no sponsored rows in the data.

The terminal carries exactly one sponsor seat and we intend to keep it that way. It covers this page, a line in the footer of every route, the project README, our posts, and the events we run. Data views stay clean, because that is the whole point of the terminal.

What the seat does not include: influence over rankings, listings, chart contents, or any placement inside a data view. That is written into the charter and it is not negotiable.
